Meteorological Seasons

Spring is a season of renewal, growth, and new beginnings, marked by warmer weather, longer days, and blooming plants.

Summer is a warm season with longer days and dry weather, perfect for outdoor activities and vacations. It is associated with relaxation and a more laid-back lifestyle, but can also bring about challenges such as droughts and wildfires in some regions.

Autumn is a season of cooler temperatures, shorter days, and colorful foliage. It is associated with harvest, abundance, and a time of transition. People enjoy outdoor activities such as hiking and apple picking, and it is celebrated for its beauty and changing colors.

Winter is a cold and snowy season with shorter days. It offers opportunities for outdoor activities like skiing and snowboarding, and is associated with holidays and cozy indoor activities. However, it can also bring about challenges such as blizzards and power outages.
